**Calories** allows you to see your energy balance and nutrition intake in the widget.
You can check if you have taken too much energy or nutrition from food.

![](materials/screenshots_v3.png)

## Requirements

- Xcode 13.0+
- macOS 12.0+
- iOS 15.0+
